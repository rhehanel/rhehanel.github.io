Tigergang

☀︎ 2026

The ghost I know is a memory forgotten through trauma. What has been forgotten comes back as an alien apparition. Those who flee from the apparition and manage to unsee it end up losing the ability to sense. And they become vampires, zombies, tin men. The tiger used to live alongside humans. It lived in a house with walls of human skin and a roof of human hair. Now the tiger has lost its territory, drifting away. The human is not a being but a site of endless division. The one who lost the tiger is a place living as the dead.

We always walk on the past, present, and future at once. Amid forgetting, catastrophe returns with a different face each time; all things sicken slowly. While the shock is repeating within spread, accumulation, and forgetting, everyone has stuck to death without knowing what death is. How do we bring the tiger back into the house?

There are too many ghosts here now; I can't tell if this is the world of the living or the dead. I guess a ritual is needed.
